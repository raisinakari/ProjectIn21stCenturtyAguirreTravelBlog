What is Avilon Zoo?

It is a facility where animals are kept within enclosures for public exhibition and often for breeding and conservation purposes. Zoos aim to provide a safe environment for animals while educating the public about wildlife. Many zoos now focus on conservation efforts, including breeding programs for endangered species

Where does Avilon Zoo Located?

Avilon Zoo is a 7.5-hectare (19-acre) zoo located in Barrio San Isidro, Rodriguez, Rizal, Philippines. It is operated by the Avilon Wildlife Conservation Foundation, which also operated the Ark Avilon Zoo in Pasig.
